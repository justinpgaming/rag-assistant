Redstone dust carries a signal up to 15 blocks.

Redstone repeaters can extend the signal beyond 15 blocks and introduce delay.

Repeaters have 4 delay settings, measured in redstone ticks.

Observers detect block updates and emit a short redstone pulse.

Comparators can compare signal strength or read container contents.

Pistons can push and pull blocks when powered by redstone.

Sticky pistons pull blocks back when the signal is removed.
